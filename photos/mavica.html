Sony Mavica FD-75

the Sony Mavica FD-75 is a floppy disk digital camera released by Sony in 2001

i bought mine second hand in august 2019 and i've since become so enamored with it that i started going by "mavica" around the same time

it takes 640x480 pixels and on a 1.44MB floppy you can fit around 20 JPEG compressed images
