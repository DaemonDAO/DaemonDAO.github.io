Good for PaintSwap?

With PaintSwap's imminent cross-chain plans, landing with a bang and capturing trading volume early-on is vital. We believe the Sweepers can help:

  • Attract listings and therefore volume
  • Court users with the prospect of sustained volume (higher likelihood of listings selling)
  • Retain users by keeping them regularly engaged (via voting)

Good for Daemons

So why does this help Daemons?

As a relatively small project with no proper backing, we want more than anything move beyond being seen as a purely art-focused NFT project

We do not expect to make a killing off of the team cut, but having our name on an autonomous utility collection is exactly the kind of optics we're aiming for.
